ABOUT Andab Care Home Health

At Andab Care Home Health, located in Westerville, OH, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional home health care services tailored to each client’s unique needs. With over ten years of experience in medical home care, we provide personalized support that allows individuals to remain safe and comfortable in their own homes.

Our caregiver services include skilled nursing care, physical Therapy, occupational therapy, personal care and home making services. Whether you or your loved one needs assistance with ambulation, bathing, dressing, exercise, feeding, hair care, oral care, nail care, positioning, shaving, toileting, transfers, or medication reminders, our dedicated team is here to help. We accept both private pay and Medicaid, ensuring our services are accessible to all who need them. Our office is open Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and we are committed to providing compassionate care when you need it most.
